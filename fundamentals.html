Module 1: Engineering Design Process

Description:

  • Making problem-solving easier by thinking like an engineer
  • Seperating a design process into digestible stages
    • Identify the problem (so it doesn't bite you later)
    • Research the problem (so you are informed)
    • Brainstorm solutions
    • Plan a solution (to visualize what you're going to build)
    • Create/Build a prototype
    • Test the prototype
    • Improve what didn't work
    • Share the results
  • Estimated time: 5 minutes

Module 2: SPIKE Prime and LEGO Geometry

Description:

  • Owning your kit
  • Identifying sensors and important sections in LEGO Spike Prime
    • The gyro sensor
    • The color sensor
    • The distance sensor (ultrasonic)
    • The force sensor
  • Look deeper into what each type of pin does
    • The friction pin
    • The non-friction pin
  • Estimated time: 10 minutes

Module 4: Gears

Description:

  • Identifying the "driver gear" and "follower gear"

Module 5: Gear Ratios

Description:

  • Exploring the idea of gear ratios and what they represent
  • Understanding the types of gear ratios that exist
    • Torque
    • Speed
  • Gaining intuition on how torque and speed are created
  • Understanding the strengths of torque and speed
  • Applying the right gear ratio to a tool or frame
