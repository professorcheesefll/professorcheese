Module 1: Engineering Design Process

Description:

  • Making problem solving easier by thinking like an engineer
  • Seperating a design process into digestible stages
    • Identify the problem (so it doesn't bite you later)
    • Research the problem (so you are informed)
    • Brainstorm solutions
    • Plan a solution (to visualize what you're going to build)
    • Create/Build a prototype
    • Test the prototype
    • Improve what didn't work
    • Share the results
  • Estimated time: 5 minutes
