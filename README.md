# Professor Cheese: A First Lego League Challenge Robotics Website

Professor Cheese is a free-to-use website with high quality robotics education. It is meant to fill a gap: Make FLL education for new engineers free, with a huge library of free-to-download Studio 2.0 files.

## What is Included?

- 30+ Studio 2.0 CAD files in the fundamentals section
- 10 structured learning modules to teach all the fundamentals of FLL engineering
- "Robots", "Tools", and "Frames" sections with libraries of Studio 2.0 CAD files

## Who is it For?

- The fundamentals modules are for beginner or intermediate teams
- The CAD libraries are for advanced teams as well, as they can be used to brainstorm ideas
- Youth Mentors and Coaches

## Status

- Actively maintained by a former competitive FLL Alumni and Youth Mentor
