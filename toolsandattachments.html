Module 10: Tools and Attachments

In this last module, you'll build tools. It's not something a resource like Professor Cheese can give to you. Instead, this module will show you CAD models of some common archetypes of tools and attachments.

What's a Good Tool or Attachment Look Like?

A good tool or attachment does:

  • Its job without having to "look good".
  • Takes a very short amount of time to place on the robot.
  • Completes a task in a short period of time. Remember. You only have 2:30 during the robot game.

Most tools are a combination of these 3 archetypes and more. Yes, that's right. There are more, but the ones here are meant to be a representation. Good strategy involves using the same tool for a mission with a similar function to solve it.
