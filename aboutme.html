About Professor Cheese

Professor Cheese is a sizzling slice of cheddar on a mission: whenever confusion meets contraptions, he jumps in to help. On this website, he guides students through FLL engineering (mechanical fundamentals), Studio 2.0 CAD, and hands-on learning.

About the Creator

Created and maintained by Annaji Garimella, a youth mentor and former competitive FIRST LEGO League participant. This website was built to help beginner and intermediate FLL teams learn core engineering fundamentals through free, open-access educational resources and downloadable Studio 2.0 CAD files.
